Crystal deployment documentation has been removed from Scalingo Documentation.

Crystal applications were documented through the official
[crystal-lang/heroku-buildpack-crystal](https://github.com/crystal-lang/heroku-buildpack-crystal)
buildpack. This upstream buildpack is archived and no longer maintained, so we
do not recommend using it for new deployments.
