github: 'https://github.com/crystal-lang/heroku-buildpack-crystal'
---

Crystal applications were documented through the external
[crystal-lang/heroku-buildpack-crystal](https://github.com/crystal-lang/heroku-buildpack-crystal)
buildpack. This upstream buildpack has been archived on the 7th of January
2025 and is no longer maintained.

As a result, Crystal deployment documentation has been removed from Scalingo
Documentation and we do not recommend using this buildpack for new deployments.
