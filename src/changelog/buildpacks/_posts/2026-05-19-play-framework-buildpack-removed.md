title: 'Play Framework - Deprecation'
github: 'https://github.com/Scalingo/play-buildpack'
---

The [play-buildpack](https://github.com/Scalingo/play-buildpack), used for
legacy Play 1.x applications, has been deprecated and archived on the 24th of
December 2025.

Play 2 and later applications remain supported through the regular
[Java]({% post_url languages/java/2000-01-01-start %}) or
[Scala]({% post_url languages/scala/2000-01-01-start %}) buildpacks, depending
on your project.

We won't provide any support for Play 1.x applications anymore.
