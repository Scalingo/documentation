---

The legacy Play Framework buildpack for Play 1.x applications has been removed
and is no longer provided by Scalingo.

Play 2 and later applications remain supported through the regular
[Java]({% post_url languages/java/2000-01-01-start %}) or
[Scala]({% post_url languages/scala/2000-01-01-start %}) buildpacks, depending
on your project.
