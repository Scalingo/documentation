PHP `8.5.0` is now available!

Due to breaking changes, the following extensions are **not** available yet
with PHP `8.5.0`:
- [`amqp`](https://github.com/php-amqp/php-amqp)
- [`igbinary`](https://github.com/igbinary/igbinary)
- [`imagick`](https://github.com/Imagick/imagick)

- [PHP 8.5.0](https://www.php.net/ChangeLog-8.php#8.5.0)
