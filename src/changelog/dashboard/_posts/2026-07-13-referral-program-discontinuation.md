title: 'Referral program discontinuation'
---

As part of the rollout of Organizations, we are discontinuing the Scalingo referral program.

The current referral mechanism is not compatible with the new account model. We will evaluate whether a new referral program can be introduced in the future as part of the Organizations experience.

If you have questions or a specific situation to discuss, please contact our
[Sales team](https://scalingo.com/book-a-demo).
