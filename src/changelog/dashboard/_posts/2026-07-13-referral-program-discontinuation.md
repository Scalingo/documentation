title: 'Referral program discontinuation'
---

We are discontinuing the Scalingo Referral Program because it no longer meets our expectations and is not aligned with upcoming changes to the platform.

**From 1 August 2026, no new referral credits can be earned**. Any referral credits you have already earned will continue to be automatically applied to invoices covering usage **up to 31 December 2026**.

If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact our Support team.
